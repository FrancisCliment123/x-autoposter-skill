# X/Twitter Growth Playbook for @wealthmaiaAI

## 1. ALGORITHM — What Controls Your Reach

The algorithm scores every tweet with this formula:

| Action | Weight |
|--------|--------|
| Retweet | **20x** |
| Reply | **13.5x** |
| Profile click | **12x** |
| Link click | **11x** |
| Bookmark | **10x** |
| Like | **1x** |

**Critical**: The first 30-60 minutes decide everything. A tweet with 10 replies in 15 minutes crushes one with 10 replies over 24 hours.

**Reach killers**: External links in tweets get **50-90% reach reduction**. Never put links in the main tweet body.

**X Premium**: Gives **2-4x visibility boost**. Non-premium needs 4-8x the engagement for same reach. Get it.

## 2. POSTING STRATEGY

- **3-5 tweets/day**, spaced 2-3 hours apart
- **2 threads/week** (highest bookmark rate = algorithm gold)
- **Best times**: 8-10 AM EST, 12-2 PM EST, 5-7 PM EST (Tue-Thu best)
- Post at the **same 3 times daily** = 40% more consistent engagement
- Weekends: 1-2 tweets max, lighter content

## 3. CONTENT MIX (The 80/15/5 Rule)

- **80% value** — insights, tips, frameworks about wealth, AI, fintech
- **15% personality** — build-in-public, behind the scenes, founder stories
- **5% promo** — WealthMaia mentions, always soft

### Content Pillars for @wealthmaiaAI:
1. **AI + finance insights** (what AI changes about wealth management)
2. **Money tips** (practical wealth advice that shows expertise)
3. **Build-in-public** (revenue milestones, user stories, challenges)
4. **Industry hot takes** (contrarian fintech opinions)
5. **Curated knowledge** (breakdowns of trends, papers, news)

## 4. HOOK FORMULAS THAT WORK

```
"Most [common belief] is wrong. Here's what actually works:"
"I spent [X time] studying [topic]. [Number] lessons:"
"Stop doing [common practice]. Do this instead:"
"[Specific number] changed everything about [topic]:"
"Nobody talks about this, but [surprising insight]"
"[Bold claim]. Let me explain:"
"The difference between [X] and [Y]:"
```

**Rules**: Front-load the most interesting part into the first 8 words. You have 0.3 seconds to stop the scroll.

## 5. LINK STRATEGY (Never Kill Your Reach)

Links in tweets = 50-90% reach penalty. Instead:

1. **Link in bio** — Pin a tweet saying "Free guide in bio"
2. **Link in reply** — Post the tweet, then reply to yourself with the link
3. **"Comment X and I'll DM you"** — drives replies (algorithm loves it) + captures leads
4. **Thread with CTA at end** — Value first, last tweet = soft CTA to wealthmaia.com

## 6. ENGAGEMENT STRATEGY (Non-Negotiable)

- **Reply to 10-20 accounts daily** in the fintech/AI space — thoughtful, not "great post!"
- **Quote tweet** bigger accounts with your own insight added
- **Reply to your own tweets** within 1 hour to boost the thread
- Engage **before AND after** you post (30 min of replies before, 30 min after)
- Target accounts with 5K-50K followers (big enough for reach, small enough to notice you)

## 7. AUTOMATION SAFETY

**Safe to automate:**
- Scheduling tweets (via API, what our skill does)
- Content generation (draft with AI, edit to sound human)

**NEVER automate:**
- Likes, follows, retweets, replies, DMs — instant flag
- Identical text to multiple users
- More than 10 posts/day

**Safe patterns:**
- 3-5 posts/day via API
- Space 2-3 hours apart
- Randomize timing slightly (+/- 15 min)
- Always edit AI-generated text to sound natural
- Never post duplicate content

## 8. PROMOTING WEALTHMAIA WITHOUT BEING SALESY

**The "Value Sandwich":**
1. Tweet a genuinely useful finance tip
2. Mention "this is why I built WealthMaia" naturally
3. No link, no CTA — let curiosity do the work

**Soft CTA examples:**
- "This is exactly what we're solving at WealthMaia (link in bio if curious)"
- "Comment 'WEALTH' and I'll show you how our AI handles this"
- Thread: 7 tweets of pure value, tweet 8: "We built a tool that does all of this automatically"

**Pinned tweet**: Your best thread with a CTA to wealthmaia.com at the end

## 9. GROWTH TIMELINE

**Month 1 (0-500 followers):**
- Post 3x/day, engage 30+ min/day
- Focus on replies to bigger accounts in fintech
- Build-in-public content

**Month 2-3 (500-2K):**
- Add 2 threads/week
- Start quote-tweeting with insights
- Pin a high-performing thread

**Month 3-6 (2K-10K):**
- Consistent 5x/day
- Collaborate with other founders
- Launch viral thread series ("How I built X from 0 to Y")
