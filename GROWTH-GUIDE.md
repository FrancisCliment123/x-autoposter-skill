# X/Twitter Growth Playbook — Personal Founder Account Strategy

**Core principle**: You are a PERSON building in public, not a product account doing promo. Your name goes on the account. Wealthmaia lives in the bio and in 5% of your posts.

## 1. ALGORITHM — What Controls Your Reach

| Action | Weight |
|--------|--------|
| Retweet | **20x** |
| Reply | **13.5x** |
| Profile click | **12x** |
| Link click | **11x** |
| Bookmark | **10x** |
| Like | **1x** |

**Critical**: The first 30-60 minutes decide everything. A tweet with 10 replies in 15 minutes crushes one with 10 replies over 24 hours.

**Reach killers**: External links in tweets get **50-90% reach reduction**. Never put links in the main tweet body.

**X Premium**: Gives **2-4x visibility boost** and ~600 impressions/post vs ~60 for free (Buffer data, 18M posts). Worth it at $8/mo BUT only after fixing content + engagement. Premium amplifies what already works — it doesn't create success from nothing.

## 2. THE 70/30 RULE (Most Important Section)

**70% of your X time = ENGAGEMENT (replies, discussions, DMs)**
**30% of your X time = CREATING new content**

Replies are the #1 growth lever at 0 followers. When you reply to an account with 10K-100K followers, thousands of people see your reply. If it adds value, they check your profile. If your profile is optimized, they follow.

### Reply targeting:
- **5K-50K follower accounts** in wealth/fintech/personal finance — big enough for reach, small enough to notice you
- **Reply with substance**, not "great post!" — add a data point, contrarian angle, or personal experience
- **15x algo weight** for replies vs 1x for likes — the algorithm WANTS you to reply
- **10-20 replies/day** minimum (mix of manual + autopilot)

### Reply rules:
- Reference something specific from their tweet
- Add genuine value or a new angle
- Keep under 200 characters
- ZERO product mentions — the funnel is: reply → profile → bio → click
- Never start with "As a..." or generic filler

## 3. CONTENT STRATEGY (95/5 Rule)

- **95% value** — insights, tips, build-in-public updates, behind-the-scenes, honest mistakes, hot takes
- **5% soft product mentions** — and ONLY using the Value Sandwich technique

This is different from the old 80/15/5. Research shows personal founder accounts that barely mention their product get MORE clicks to it than accounts that promote constantly. Curiosity > promotion.

### Content Pillars:
1. **Build-in-public** — MRR updates (even if $0), user counts, shipped features, honest failures
2. **Wealth/finance insights** — practical tips that show expertise (what your product is built on)
3. **Founder journey** — decisions, tradeoffs, lessons, the human side of building
4. **Hot takes** — contrarian opinions about fintech, AI, wealth management
5. **Curated knowledge** — breakdowns of trends, papers, news in your space

### What performs best (2026 data):
- **Threads** → highest bookmark rate = algorithm gold
- **Screenshots** of real data (MRR, signups, code, UI) → authenticity signal
- **Contrarian takes** → highest reply rate → highest algo score
- **Vulnerability posts** (mistakes, failures) → highest profile click rate

## 4. POSTING STRATEGY

- **3-5 tweets/day**, spaced 2-3 hours apart
- **2 threads/week** (highest engagement format)
- **Best times**: 8-10 AM EST, 12-2 PM EST, 5-7 PM EST (Tue-Thu best)
- Post at **same 3 times daily** = 40% more consistent engagement
- Weekends: 1-2 tweets max, lighter content

## 5. HOOK FORMULAS THAT WORK

```
"Most [common belief] is wrong. Here's what actually works:"
"I spent [X time] studying [topic]. [Number] lessons:"
"Stop doing [common practice]. Do this instead:"
"[Specific number] changed everything about [topic]:"
"Nobody talks about this, but [surprising insight]"
"[Bold claim]. Let me explain:"
"The difference between [X] and [Y]:"
"Week [N] building my SaaS from zero:"
```

Front-load the most interesting part into the first 8 words. You have 0.3 seconds to stop the scroll.

## 6. LINK STRATEGY (Never Kill Your Reach)

Links in tweets = 50-90% reach penalty. Instead:

1. **Link in bio** — Pin a tweet saying "link in bio"
2. **Link in reply** — Post the tweet, then reply to yourself with the link
3. **"Comment X and I'll DM you"** — drives replies (algo loves it) + captures leads
4. **Thread with CTA at end** — Value first, last tweet = soft CTA

## 7. PROMOTING WEALTHMAIA WITHOUT BEING SALESY

**The Value Sandwich:**
1. Tweet a genuinely useful finance insight
2. Mention "this is why I'm building [product]" naturally
3. No link, no CTA — let curiosity do the work

**Soft CTA examples:**
- "This is exactly the problem I'm solving with my wealth app (link in bio if curious)"
- "Comment 'WEALTH' and I'll show you how we handle this"
- Thread: 7 tweets of pure value, tweet 8: "I built a tool that does all of this automatically"

**Pinned tweet**: Your best thread about a wealth insight, with a soft CTA at the end.

**Product mention frequency**: Max 1 in 20 tweets. The other 19 are pure value.

## 8. AUTOMATION SAFETY + SHADOWBAN PREVENTION

### Safe to automate (via official API):
- Scheduling tweets (what post.js does)
- Content generation (draft with AI, always unique)
- Strategic replies via autopilot (unique, contextual, value-adding, max 5/day)
- Analytics and monitoring

### Shadowban triggers to AVOID:
- More than 10 automated interactions/day
- Identical or near-identical text across posts
- Perfectly timed posts (exact same minute every day)
- Aggressive following/unfollowing
- Using unauthorized tools that bypass OAuth
- Replying with generic text ("Great post!", "Agreed!")

### Safe patterns:
- 3-5 posts/day via API
- Max 5 auto-replies/day (each unique and contextual)
- Space 2-3 hours apart
- Randomize timing ±15 minutes
- Always unique content — never duplicate
- Mix autopilot replies with manual engagement

### If you get shadowbanned:
1. **Stop ALL automation immediately** for 7-14 days
2. Check at shadowban.eu
3. Post 1-2 manual, high-quality tweets per day
4. Do 5-10 manual replies per day (genuine conversations)
5. Wait for recovery (typically 2-14 days)
6. Resume automation at 50% previous rate, increase gradually

## 9. GROWTH TIMELINE (Realistic)

**Month 1 (0-300 followers):**
- Post 3x/day, engage 30+ min/day
- 70% time on replies to bigger accounts
- Build-in-public content (even with 0 users — document the building)
- Get X Premium after week 2

**Month 2-3 (300-1K followers):**
- Add 2 threads/week
- Start quote-tweeting with insights
- Pin a high-performing thread
- Autopilot running 2-3x/day

**Month 3-6 (1K-5K followers):**
- Consistent 5x/day
- Collaborate with other founders (quote each other)
- Launch viral thread series ("How I built X from 0 to Y")
- Compounding starts — growth accelerates

**Month 6+ (5K+):**
- You have distribution. Product mentions start converting
- DM strategy for high-intent followers
- Consider Wealthmaia-specific thread series

## 10. PROFILE OPTIMIZATION

- **Name**: Your real name (not Wealthmaia)
- **Bio**: "Building [Wealthmaia] — [1-line value prop]. Sharing the journey from $0 to $X. [personal touch]"
- **Link**: wealthmaia.com (or waitlist)
- **Pinned tweet**: Your best value thread with soft CTA
- **Profile pic**: Your face (not a logo)
- **Banner**: Can include product branding subtly
